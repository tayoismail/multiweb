Tip Calculator — Calculate Tips and Split Bills Easily

Our Tip Calculator makes calculating tips and splitting bills effortless. Whether you're dining solo, with a partner, or with a large group, this tool instantly computes the tip amount, total bill, and per-person cost. No more awkward mental math or fumbling with your phone at the table.


Choose from common tip percentages (10%, 15%, 18%, 20%, 25%) or enter a custom percentage. Split the bill between up to 100 people, and optionally round up the total to the nearest dollar for cleaner splitting. The tool updates in real-time as you type, so you always see the current numbers.

How to Use the Tip Calculator

  1. Enter the bill amountType the total bill amount before tip (e.g., $85.50).
  2. Select tip percentageChoose from the preset buttons or click "Custom" to enter your own percentage.
  3. Split the billEnter how many people are splitting the bill. The per-person amount updates automatically.
  4. Optional: Round upChoose to round the total to the nearest dollar or round up for easier splitting.

Frequently Asked Questions

Standard tipping in the US: 15-20% at restaurants, 15% for takeout, $1-2 per drink at bars, 15-20% for haircuts, and $2-5 for delivery. In countries where tipping isn't customary (Japan, Australia), it's generally not expected.

Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax amount. However, many people tip on the post-tax total for convenience. Both approaches are acceptable — the difference is usually small.

Use this calculator to split evenly, or for fairness, each person can calculate their own portion based on what they ordered. Apps like Venmo or Zelle make it easy to collect individual amounts. Consider designating one person to pay and having everyone else reimburse them.
