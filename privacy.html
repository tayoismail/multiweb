Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Here's how we protect it.

Last updated: July 9, 2026

Privacy-First by Design

MultiWeb is built on a simple principle: your data stays with you. All of our tools run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Nothing you type, calculate, or upload ever leaves your device. We have no servers that process your data, no databases storing your inputs, and no analytics tracking your behavior.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information — We don't ask for your name, email, or any identifying data
  • No usage tracking — We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts
  • No cookies for tracking — The only cookie we use stores your theme preference (light/dark mode) locally
  • No server-side processing — All calculations, conversions, and image processing happen in your browser
  • No data storage — We don't store any of your inputs, results, or uploaded files on our servers

How Our Tools Work

Every MultiWeb tool is a static HTML page with embedded JavaScript. When you use a tool:

  • Your browser downloads the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript files once
  • All processing happens locally using your device's CPU
  • No data is sent to any server during tool usage
  • When you close the page, all data is gone — nothing persists

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — We load the Inter font from Google's CDN. Google may collect your IP address when the font is loaded. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • QR Code library (QRious) — The QR Generator loads a small JavaScript library from a CDN for QR code rendering. No data is sent to the CDN provider.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our Contact page or email us at hello@multiweb.app.
