Net Worth Calculator — Calculate Your Total Net Worth

Net worth is the single most important number in personal finance — it's the value of everything you own minus everything you owe. Our Net Worth Calculator helps you get a clear snapshot of your financial health by adding up all your assets (cash, investments, property) and subtracting all your liabilities (loans, mortgages, credit card debt).


Tracking your net worth over time is one of the best ways to measure financial progress. Whether you're just starting out or approaching retirement, knowing your net worth helps you make better decisions about saving, investing, and spending.

How to Use the Net Worth Calculator

  1. Enter your assetsFill in the value of cash, investments, retirement accounts, real estate, vehicles, and any other assets you own.
  2. Enter your liabilitiesInput all debts: mortgage, auto loans, student loans, credit cards, other debt, and taxes owed.
  3. Click CalculateSee your total net worth, debt-to-asset ratio, and a visual breakdown of your financial position.
