Discount Calculator — Calculate Sale Price and Savings

Our Discount Calculator makes it easy to figure out the final price after any discount. Whether you're shopping during Black Friday, using a coupon code, or taking advantage of a clearance sale, this tool instantly shows you the sale price and exactly how much money you're saving. No more mental math or guesswork at the register.


Simply enter the original price and discount percentage to see your savings instantly. Choose from common discount percentages or enter a custom value. The calculator also supports multiple quantities, so you can see the total cost when buying several discounted items.

The Discount Formula

Sale Price = Original Price × (1 - Discount% / 100)
Example: A $200 item with 25% off: Sale Price = $200 × (1 - 0.25) = $200 × 0.75 = $150 (You save $50)

How to Use the Discount Calculator

  1. Enter the original priceType the full price of the item before any discounts are applied.
  2. Select or enter the discount percentageChoose from common percentages (10%, 20%, 30%, 50%) or click "Custom" for your own value.
  3. Set the quantityIf buying multiple items, enter the quantity to see the total discounted price.
  4. View results instantlyThe sale price, savings, and total update in real-time as you type.
