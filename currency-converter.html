Frequently Asked Questions

Exchange rates in this converter are updated regularly throughout the day. However, actual rates at banks and exchange services may differ due to spreads, fees, and market conditions. Always verify rates with your financial institution before making transactions.

Banks and exchange services add a markup (spread) to the mid-market rate. They also may charge transaction fees. The rates shown here are mid-market rates, which represent the midpoint between buy and sell prices in the global currency markets.

For the best rates, use a no-foreign-transaction-fee credit card for purchases, withdraw from ATMs at your destination (avoid airport exchanges), and compare rates from different providers. Services like Wise (TransferWise) often offer rates closer to the mid-market rate.
