Create QR Codes Instantly — No Sign-Up Required

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned by smartphones and QR readers to instantly access information — URLs, contact details, WiFi credentials, plain text, and more. QR codes have become essential for businesses, marketers, event organizers, and everyday users who need a fast, scannable bridge between the physical and digital world.


Our free QR code generator lets you create fully customizable QR codes in seconds — no sign-up, no tracking, no limits. Choose your size, pick your colors, and download a high-resolution PNG ready for print or digital use. Whether you're adding a QR code to a business card, flyer, restaurant menu, presentation slide, or social media post, this tool gives you a clean, production-ready QR code with just a few clicks.


Unlike other online QR generators, we don't watermark your codes, impose download limits, or redirect through tracking servers. Everything runs directly in your browser — your data never touches a server.

How to Use the QR Code Generator

  1. Enter your content Type or paste the URL, text, phone number, or any other data you want to encode into the input field above. For example: https://example.com.
  2. Customize the appearance Choose your preferred size (Small, Medium, Large, or Extra Large) and pick custom colors for both the QR code pattern and its background using the color pickers.
  3. Generate the QR code Click the "Generate QR Code" button (or toggle Auto-generate to create codes instantly as you type). Your QR code will appear in the output area below.
  4. Scan and verify Use your phone's camera or a QR scanning app to verify the code works. Test it before printing or sharing to ensure it redirects to the correct destination.
  5. Download and share Click the "Download PNG" button to save your QR code as a high-resolution PNG image. Use it on websites, flyers, business cards, posters, menus, or anywhere you need a scannable code.
