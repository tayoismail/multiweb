About MultiWeb

Free online tools built with care for speed, privacy, and simplicity.

Our Mission

MultiWeb was created with a simple goal: provide high-quality, free online tools that work instantly in your browser — no sign-ups, no clutter, no data collection. We believe everyday tasks like converting units, counting words, or generating passwords should be quick and accessible to everyone.


Every tool on MultiWeb is built with modern web standards, runs entirely in your browser for maximum privacy, and is designed to work beautifully on both desktop and mobile devices. We prioritize speed, accuracy, and a clean user experience.

What We Offer

Our growing collection includes:

  • BMI Calculator — Calculate your Body Mass Index with metric and imperial support
  • Unit Converter — Convert between 60+ units across length, weight, temperature, volume, and speed
  • QR Code Generator — Create customizable QR codes for URLs, text, and more
  • Word Counter — Count words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs with reading time estimates
  • Percentage Calculator — Calculate percentages, discounts, tips, and percentage changes
  • Password Generator — Create cryptographically secure passwords with full customization
  • Image Converter — Convert images between PNG, JPG, and WebP — all in your browser
  • Word Unscrambler — Find valid English words from scrambled letters for word games

Privacy First

We take privacy seriously. None of our tools send your data to external servers. Everything runs locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your inputs, calculations, and files never leave your device. We don't use cookies for tracking, and we don't collect personal information.
