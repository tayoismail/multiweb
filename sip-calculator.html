SIP Returns Calculator — Calculate Mutual Fund SIP Returns Online

A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds, building wealth through the power of compounding. Whether you're a first-time investor or a seasoned market participant, our SIP Returns Calculator helps you project the future value of your investments with precision. Simply enter your monthly investment amount, expected annual return rate, and investment duration to see your potential wealth accumulation.


This tool is ideal for financial planners, young professionals starting their investment journey, and anyone looking to visualize how disciplined, small contributions can grow into substantial corpus over time. You can also factor in an annual increase in your SIP amount to simulate how stepping up your investments each year accelerates wealth creation.

The SIP Formula

FV = P × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r] × (1 + r)
Where: P = monthly investment, r = monthly rate of return (annual rate ÷ 12), n = total number of months.

Example: Investing $500/month at 12% annual return for 10 years: FV = 500 × [((1.01)^120 - 1) / 0.01] × 1.01 = $116,169

How to Use the SIP Returns Calculator

  1. Enter your monthly SIP amountType the fixed amount you plan to invest each month (e.g., $500).
  2. Input expected annual returnEnter the expected annual return rate based on historical performance or your financial advisor's recommendation (e.g., 12%).
  3. Specify investment durationEnter how many years you plan to continue the SIP (e.g., 10, 15, or 20 years).
  4. Optional: Annual SIP increaseIf you plan to increase your SIP amount each year, enter the percentage increase (e.g., 10%).
  5. Click CalculatePress the button to see your projected future value, total invested amount, and estimated returns.
