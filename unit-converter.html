Unit Converter — Convert Length, Weight, Temperature & More

Whether you're a student studying science, a DIY home renovator following an overseas tutorial, a traveler navigating foreign measurements, or a professional working with international specifications — unit conversion is an everyday necessity. Our free unit converter supports five categories (length, weight, temperature, volume, and speed) with dozens of units in each, making it easy to switch between metric, imperial, and other measurement systems.


Conversions update instantly as you type — no button pressing needed. Simply select your category, enter a value, choose the source and target units, and the converted result appears immediately. A swap button lets you reverse the conversion direction, and your recent conversions are saved to a handy history list so you can revisit them later.


Behind the scenes, every conversion uses precise formulas and conversion factors. Temperature conversions handle the different scaling (Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin), while length, weight, volume, and speed all use consistent multiplicative factors with high precision.

How to Use the Unit Converter

  1. Select a categoryClick on one of the category pills — Length, Weight, Temperature, Volume, or Speed. The unit dropdowns will update to show relevant units for that category.
  2. Enter a valueType or paste the number you want to convert in the Value field. Decimal values are supported (e.g., 1.5, 3.14).
  3. Choose unitsSelect the unit you're converting from in the first dropdown, and the unit you want to convert to in the second dropdown. Use the swap button (⇄) to quickly reverse them.
  4. View the resultThe converted value appears instantly below with full precision formatting. A conversion formula reference is shown beneath the result.
  5. Reference historyYour recent conversions are saved in the history list. Click on any history item to re-run that conversion, or click Clear all to reset.
