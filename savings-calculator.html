Savings Goal Calculator — Find How Much to Save Monthly

Our Savings Goal Calculator helps you create a concrete plan to reach any financial goal. Whether you're saving for an emergency fund, vacation, car, house down payment, or any other target, this tool shows you exactly how much you need to save each month to hit your goal on time.


Enter your target amount, current savings, target date, and expected interest rate to get a personalized savings plan. The calculator also factors in any amount you're already saving each month, so you'll know if you're on track or need to increase your contributions.

How to Use the Savings Goal Calculator

Frequently Asked Questions

Financial experts recommend saving 3-6 months of essential expenses. If you spend $3,000/month on essentials, aim for $9,000-$18,000. Start with a $1,000 mini emergency fund, then build to 3 months, and eventually 6 months.

For short-term goals (1-3 years), use a high-yield savings account (4-5% APY). For medium-term (3-5 years), consider CDs or Treasury bills. For long-term goals (5+ years), invest in index funds for potentially higher returns. Keep emergency funds easily accessible.

Try these strategies: 1) Extend your timeline (save less per month over more months), 2) Reduce your goal (save for a smaller target first), 3) Increase income (side hustles, overtime), 4) Cut expenses (reduce subscriptions, dining out). Even small amounts add up over time.
