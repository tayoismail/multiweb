Break-Even Calculator — Find Your Break-Even Point

The break-even point is the magical number where your total revenue equals your total costs — you're not making money, but you're not losing any either. Our Break-Even Calculator helps you determine exactly how many units you need to sell (and how much revenue you need to generate) to cover all your fixed and variable costs.


Whether you're launching a new product, evaluating a business idea, or setting sales targets, understanding your break-even point is essential for financial planning. The calculator also lets you set a target profit goal to see how many additional units you need to sell beyond break-even.

The Break-Even Formulas

Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs / (Price - Variable Cost per Unit)
Break-Even Revenue = Fixed Costs / Contribution Margin %
Example: Fixed costs $5,000, Price $25, Variable cost $10: Break-Even = $5,000 / ($25 - $10) = 334 units

How to Use the Break-Even Calculator

  1. Enter fixed costsSum up all costs that don't change with production volume (rent, salaries, insurance, loan payments).
  2. Set selling price per unitEnter the price you'll charge for one unit of your product or service.
  3. Enter variable cost per unitEnter the cost that varies with each unit produced (materials, packaging, direct labor).

  5. Click CalculateSee your break-even point in units and revenue, plus your contribution margin.
